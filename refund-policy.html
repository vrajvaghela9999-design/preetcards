Refund & Cancellation Policy

Commitment to Customer Satisfaction

At Preet Cards, we take pride in the quality of our craftsmanship. Because our products are highly customized to your specific needs, our refund and cancellation policies are as follows:

1. Cancellations

You may cancel your order within 24 hours of placement for a full refund, provided that the design process has not yet begun. Once design concepts have been shared or creating has started, a cancellation fee covering the work done may apply.

Once final approval is given for printing, orders cannot be cancelled.

2. Refunds and Returns

Since wedding invitations and stationery are personalized goods, we do not accept returns or offer refunds for reason of chance of mind or errors that were approved during the proofing process.

3. Defects and Errors

If there is an error on our part (e.g., the printed product differs from the approved proof) or if the goods are damaged during transit, please contact us within 3 days of receiving your order. We will reprint the affected items at no additional cost to you or offer a refund for the damaged portion.

To process a claim for damage or errors:

  • Take clear photos of the damage or error.
  • Email them to us at preetcardsvadodara@gmail.com along with your order details.
  • Return the defective items if requested.

4. Color Variations

Please note that colors may appear differently on screens than in print. Slight color variations are common in printing processes (especially gold foiling and screen printing) and are not considered defects.

5. Shipping

We are not responsible for delays caused by shipping carriers, customs clearance, or incorrect addresses provided by the customer. However, we will assist you in tracking your package and resolving delivery issues where possible.
